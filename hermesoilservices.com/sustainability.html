Corporate Social Responsibility

“We believe individual participation is critical to making a greater social impact.”

Corporate social responsibility (CSR) is a critical aspect of the business operations of Hermes. Hermes is committed to engaging in responsible and sustainable practices that benefit both the environment and society. The company strives to minimize its environmental impact by promoting the use of cleaner energy sources and implementing energy-efficient technologies in its operations.

In addition, Hermes supports various social initiatives by investing in community development programs and promoting the well-being of its employees. The company also takes measures to ensure the health and safety of its employees, customers, and the general public, by providing comprehensive HSE training and adopting strict safety protocols. By integrating CSR into its business operations, Hermes aims to make a positive contribution to society while maintaining its reputation as a socially responsible corporate citizen.

Our Areas of Focus

Diesel/Petrol Haulage & Delivery

Our company offers a range of benefits that make it a top choice for businesses across the country. These include guarantee delivery within 48 hours of receiving a request, direct deposit payment confirmation, pre and post-supply troubleshoot assistance, calibrated and certified meters, receipted supply voucher and invoices for every delivery, an experienced and capable logistics team, a nationwide distribution and plant network, and a quality assurance member escorting every delivery.

LPG Equipment Procurement & Servicing

Hermes offers a range of top-quality services and products to its valued clients, including equipment offerings from international manufacturers such as Blackmer, Corkens, Robine, and Liquid Control. We are committed to sustainable practices and offer eco-friendly equipment options. Our team is also available to provide equipment servicing and convenient sourcing and delivery options to make the process as hassle-free as possible.

Safety Training & HSE

At Hermes we take safety seriously, offering comprehensive safety protocol training and high-quality PPE to minimize the risk of injury or illness. We also offer eco-friendly products and practices whenever possible. Our team includes experienced plant operators who are trained to handle emergency safety situations and provide expert troubleshooting assistance to keep operations running smoothly and safely.
