Corporate Social Responsibility

Corporate social responsibility (CSR) refers to a company's commitment to operating in an economically, socially, and environmentally sustainable manner while contributing to the betterment of society. As an oil company, Hermes has a responsibility to minimize its environmental impact and ensure the safety of its workers and the communities where it operates.

To achieve this, the company should implement sustainable practices in its haulage, supply and distribution of petroleum products, and station & LPG plant infrastructure design and operations. Additionally, Hermes can demonstrate its commitment to CSR by providing safety practices and personal protective equipment to its employees, as well as engaging in community development initiatives and supporting local charitable causes. By prioritizing CSR, Hermes can enhance its reputation as a responsible and ethical business and contribute positively to the communities in which it operates.

Our Areas of Focus

Diesel/Petrol Haulage & Delivery

Our company offers a range of benefits that make it a top choice for businesses across the country. These include guarantee delivery within 48 hours of receiving a request, direct deposit payment confirmation, pre and post-supply troubleshoot assistance, calibrated and certified meters, receipted supply voucher and invoices for every delivery, an experienced and capable logistics team, a nationwide distribution and plant network, and a quality assurance member escorting every delivery.

LPG Equipment procurement & Servicing

Hermes offers a range of top-quality services and products to its valued clients, including equipment offerings from international manufacturers such as Blackmer, Corkens, Robine, and Liquid Control. We are committed to sustainable practices and offer eco-friendly equipment options. Our team is also available to provide equipment servicing and convenient sourcing and delivery options to make the process as hassle-free as possible.

Safety Training & HSE

At Hermes we take safety seriously, offering comprehensive safety protocol training and high-quality PPE to minimize the risk of injury or illness. Health & Safety

At Hermes, we place a high priority on the health and safety of its employees and customers. We are committed to providing safe and secure transportation of petroleum products while adhering to strict safety regulations and best practices. We always ensure that our drivers receive regular training on safe driving practices, and our vehicles are regularly inspected and maintained to prevent any accidents or incidents.

Additionally, we also supply our employees with Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) to protect them against potential hazards. Our focus on safety extends beyond just our employees, as we also provide their customers with guidelines on how to safely handle and store their products. We are dedicated to safety demonstrates our commitment to protecting both their employees and customers.
