Asharami Synergy Drives Access to Diesel with Door-2-Door Campaign

Jan 20, 2020 | Lagos, Nigeria

Share:               

Asharami Synergy has restated its commitment to providing seamless access to diesel through its door-2-door delivery initiative.

The downstream company of the energy giant, Sahara Group, launched the initiative in 2016 to promote "convenience, safety and competitive pricing in the delivery of diesel" in Lagos, Abuja and Port Harcourt.

According to Foluso Sobanjo, COO, Asharami Synergy, the company's Door-2-Door initiative ensure a hitch-free diesel supply solution to consumers, while ensuring “they receive top quality diesel delivered in the comfort of their homes or business locations. Our one litre is one litre."

Foluso said consumers can access the service placing orders via the Asharami Synergy website, www.asharamisynergy.com or call the hotline on 08159090236 to order.

He explained that Asharami had secured a wide distribution network to meet deliver orders within 12 business hours after all terms and conditions have been met by the customers.

“Asharami Synergy’s presence along the value chain of the downstream sector that includes product procurement, storage and Marketing and distribution gives us more control to ensure product availability and quality for our numerous customers. Asharami Synergy continues to invest in human capital development, technology and quality management systems to ensure customers enjoy premium service across all our businesses,” he said.
