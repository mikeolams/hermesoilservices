Projects

Hermes is proud to have successfully completed several infrastructure design, construction, and operation projects related to petroleum stations and LPG plants in Osogbo, Ondo, and Ring Road Ibadan. Our team of experts has a proven track record of delivering top-quality projects on time and within budget.

Our focus on safety, efficiency, and environmental sustainability has enabled us to design and construct state-of-the-art facilities that meet the highest standards of quality and compliance.

We work closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and develop custom solutions that best suit their requirements. We are committed to delivering outstanding results that exceed our clients' expectations and contribute to the growth and development of the petroleum and energy sectors in Nigeria.
